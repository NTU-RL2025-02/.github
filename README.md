## About Our Team

We are **NTU-RL2025-02**, a research and development team focused on Reinforcement Learning (RL) at National Taiwan University (NTU). Our team is dedicated to advancing the field of RL through innovative research, practical implementations, and collaborative projects.

### Our Mission

To explore cutting-edge reinforcement learning techniques and apply them to real-world problems, contributing to both academic research and practical applications in artificial intelligence.

### Focus Areas

- Reinforcement Learning algorithms and methodologies
- Deep Reinforcement Learning applications
- Multi-agent systems
- RL in robotics and control systems
- Policy optimization and evaluation

### Team Organization

This repository contains shared GitHub configurations, workflows, and templates used across our organization's projects. It helps maintain consistency and best practices across all team repositories.
